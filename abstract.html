Abstract

This community survey project aims to explore opinions on electric vehicles (EVs) as a daily mode of transportation. Electric vehicles have gained significant attention in recent years due to their potential to address environmental concerns and reduce dependency on fossil fuels. This survey seeks to gather data on the general perceptions, attitudes, and barriers that individuals may have towards adopting EVs for their daily commute. By understanding public opinions, we can identify key factors influencing the acceptance and uptake of electric vehicles, ultimately informing policymakers and stakeholders in promoting sustainable transportation options
