Electric Vehicles Usage In NELLORE

About Us

Welcome to our website! We are excited to share with you the findings of our recent survey on the "Usage of Electric Vehicles for Daily Mode of Transportation" in the Mansoor Nagar and Kottamitta areas of Nellore.

we believe in the power of sustainable transportation and its potential to shape a cleaner and greener future. With this survey, we aimed to gain insights into the adoption and usage patterns of electric vehicles (EVs) in these specific areas.

Our survey was conducted over a span of 1 Month and surveyed over 50 participants from various backgrounds and age groups. Through a combination of questionnaires and interviews, we sought to understand the factors influencing the decision to switch to electric vehicles, the overall experience of EV owners, and the challenges faced in the adoption process.
